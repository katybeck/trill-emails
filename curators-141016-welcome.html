It was great to meet you at MIT Hacking Arts last weekend. The whole Trill team was delighted to participate in such a creative gathering of artists and entrepreneurs! Special congratulations are in order for Cinthia Chen, who won our drawing for a pair of tickets to see a show this month.

During the festival many of you shared a word or an anecdote along with your contact info: live shows made you feel "boundless," "alive," "connected to others," and "vitally connected to the best of humanity." We wholeheartedly agree.

Trill sends one email a week filled with shows hand-picked by our savvy team of curators — Chris, Sarah, Mali, and Mary — who curate rock and experimental music, classical music, avant-garde music, and comedy respectively. They are impressive as performers and as people, and they are seriously hooked in to the Boston arts community. They'll help you find awesome and unique shows you wouldn't find otherwise.
